WELCOME TO CSV TO EXCEL CONVERTER PRO

This application is designed to convert trading CSV files into
professional Excel reports with detailed statistics.

Key Features:
- Convert CSV trading logs into Excel (.xlsx)
- Automatic summary calculation:
  * Total trades
  * Win rate
  * Profit & loss
  * Profit factor
- Separate sheets:
  ALL_TRADES, BUY_TRADES, SELL_TRADES, SUMMARY
- Colored profit and loss formatting
- Works fully offline (no internet required)

System Requirements:
- Windows 10 or later
- Python environment (bundled with installer if applicable)
- No internet connection required for operation

- Please close other running applications
